ABOUT US

CIC is a fast-growing innovative I.T. company predominantly servicing Japanese companies with an approach towards high productivity and high quality in the emerging software technologies.

We are a subsidiary of a Japanese Information Technology company offering futuristic services and solutions in various fields of Web Applications, Mobile Apps, Artificial Intelligence, Server database management, Manufacturing Execution System Software Integration, Japanese Endowed Course Training, and Consulting.

We are an ISO-27001 and ISO-9001 certified company providing utmost importance to Information Security and Quality Management too. We provide a lot of focus on the Processes and also on upskilling our Resources to match in line with the global trend. We have imbibed the culture of being Process-oriented and Quality-oriented from our Japanese parent company. The five important Mantras we focus on are – Productivity, Quality, Delivery, Skills, and Culture.

We believe that our highly skilled Software Engineers of India can collaborate well with high-quality Japanese technologies to bring out the best innovative solutions. Our dedicated skilled Software Engineers have been consistently delivering innovative solutions to the complex software requirements of our esteemed clients.
